About Us

Kickass Support Services is based in Rockhampton. We service Central Queensland and Beyond. Our clients come from all walks of life and ages and from all over Australia.

Our Team

Hello, my name is Rodney and I am the manager of KickAss Support Services. I have years of experience in the community sector in Australia as well as overseas in South Africa. I have managed, mentored and lead teams in community development. I have also worked as a Primary School Chaplain, Youth Worker, Support Worker, Assistant Coordinator, Disability Employment Consultant, Program Coordinator, Support Coordinator, I also own and run Rockhampton Martial Arts Academy (RMAA). I have 28 years of doing Martial Arts and I love every minute of it.
